Crystal Collector Game

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.There will be exactly 4 crystals. By clicking on one of the crystal it wil give you a random value to add to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number shown.You lose if your total score is above the random number.The value of eeach individual crystal has a random number.Each time the game starts the value of each crystal will change.
